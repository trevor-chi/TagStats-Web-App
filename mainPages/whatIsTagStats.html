What Is TagStats?

TagStats brings your baseball cards to life. Using NFC technology, you can unlock player stats, digital content, and interactive experiences directly from your cards.

  • Digital Integration: Scan your NFC-enabled cards to access online stats and player pages.
  • Collection Tracking: Make an account to keep track of your NFC card collection.
  • Visualizations: Dive into player achievements with interactive data visualizations that bring stats to life.

How Do NFC Baseball Cards Work?

Our NFC baseball cards are embedded with tiny chips that store digital information. Simply tap your card against an NFC-enabled device to be taken to that player's TagStats webpage!

1. Tap to Scan

Use your phone's NFC reader to scan the card. No apps required!

2. Unlock Content

Access player stats, highlight videos, and exclusive digital collectibles.

3. Track Your Collection

Log your collection on TagStats!
