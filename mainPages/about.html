About Me

Hi, I'm Trevor Childers, the creator behind TagStats. I'm currently a senior majoring in Creative Technologies and Computer Science at Berry College in Rome, GA. I've played baseball for fifteen years and collected cards for most of my life.

While collecting cards, I always sensed there was more potential waiting to be unlocked. After diving into various technologies through my education, I knew I wanted to blend my passion for baseball with my love for technology, and thus TagStats was born.
